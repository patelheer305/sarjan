Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Welcome to Sarjan ("we", "our", or "us"). We highly value your trust and are fully committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how your information is handled when you use our 1D material cut optimization application on the Google Play Store.

1. Information Collection and Use

Sarjan is designed with your absolute privacy in mind.

  • No Personal Data: We do not collect, request, require, or store any personal information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or account details.
  • Local Inputs: All measurement data, stock inventory figures, saw kerf sizes, and required cut values you input are processed entirely locally on your device.
  • Storage: Data is temporarily saved inside your device's web storage (localStorage) purely to keep your inputs intact if you refresh or reopen the app. This data never leaves your device.

2. PDF Generation & Permissions

The app features a "Download PDF Report" functionality. This document generation takes place completely client-side in your app environment using JavaScript. It does not send your data to external servers to build the PDF document.

3. Google Play Services & Third-Party Tools

Since our application is distributed via the Google Play Store, it uses Google Play Services which may collect standard device configurations or anonymous analytics info required for stability monitoring, crash logging, and app updates. For more info, please check the Google Privacy Policy.

4. Data Security

Because all your core design inputs and calculations are computed completely offline and locked within your local device container, there is no transmission path over the open internet for malicious third parties to intercept.

5. Children's Privacy

These services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may modify or update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any modifications. Changes are fully effective immediately upon publishing.
